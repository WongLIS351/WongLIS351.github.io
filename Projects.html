TV Show Poster Project:



*Shard of Darkness

In one of my courses at UW-Madison, I was assigned to use Adobe photoshop to make a poster of an imaginary TV show. I familiarized myself with overlay, opacity, vector images, and many more tools to create this poster. In this show, the main character Mia Jones, acts as a detective to find out who murdered her younger sister, and why the local police cover it up.
